The Early Career Visualization Research Group exists to let junior researchers share information and discuss important, VIS-related topics. These topics include, but are not limited to:

  • Managing a research lab or group
  • Funding opportunities and advice
  • Teaching advice and discussion
  • Connecting undergraduate students with research opportunities
  • Publication venues and opportunities (e.g., CFPs)
    • call for papers)

We use two methods for communication: a Slack channel and a mailing list. If you are interested in joining, please contact Carlos, Enrico, Petra, or Chris.

We have put forth a set of rules and guidelines to try and ensure a healthy culture for discussion among peers. Please take a moment to read through them.
